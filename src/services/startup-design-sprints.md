## Service Overview

Startup Design Sprints are intensive, 5-day programs that help startups and innovation teams rapidly move from problem identification to tested prototype. Based on Google Ventures' proven methodology, these sprints compress months of traditional development into a single week of focused collaboration and validation.

## What's Included

### 5-Day Sprint Process

- **Day 1: Map** - Define the problem and choose a target
- **Day 2: Sketch** - Generate solution ideas individually
- **Day 3: Decide** - Choose the best ideas and create storyboards
- **Day 4: Prototype** - Build a realistic prototype
- **Day 5: Test** - Validate with real users

### Deliverables

- Validated product concept
- High-fidelity interactive prototype
- User testing insights and recommendations
- Technical feasibility assessment
- Go-to-market strategy framework
- Next steps roadmap

### Post-Sprint Support

- Implementation planning
- Technical architecture guidance
- MVP development roadmap
- Investor presentation materials

## Sprint Methodology

### Google Ventures Framework

Our sprints follow the proven GV methodology, adapted for startup needs:
